How to Become a mongoori Driver

1

Check Your Eligibility

To drive with mongoori, you'll need:

  • Valid California Driver's License (current, in good standing)
  • Clean driving record — no major violations in the past 3 years
  • Smartphone access (iOS or Android) to use the driver app
  • Age 21 or older (legal requirement for commercial driving)
  • Work authorization in the United States
Time Required: ~2 minutes to verify
2

Complete Your Application

The application is quick and straightforward:

  • Fill out your profile — name, contact info, driving history
  • Provide driver's license info (photo and license number)
  • Consent to a professional background check
  • Choose your payment preference (daily, weekly, or monthly)
Time Required: 5–10 minutes
3

Get Approved & Trained

Once you submit, here's what happens:

  • Background review by our team — usually within 24 hours
  • Payment setup for direct deposits to your bank
  • Short orientation video on mongoori policies and the app (10 min)
  • Optional onboarding call with the support team
  • Vehicle assignment — you're matched with a Tesla near your location
Approval Timeline: 24–48 hours
4

Start Earning

You're ready to drive:

  • Download the mongoori Driver App (iOS and Android)
  • Log in with your new driver credentials
  • Check your assigned Tesla's location in the app
  • Toggle to "Available" and start accepting ride requests
  • Earnings deposited directly to your bank account
Time to First Ride: Same day or next business day after vehicle pickup

How to Get Started with Your Tesla

Once approved, here's the simple process to get your vehicle and start earning:

1

Browse Available Vehicles

Log into the mongoori Driver Portal and explore available Tesla models near you:

  • View all available vehicles in your service area (LA, Orange County, Irvine, San Diego)
  • Check vehicle specs, mileage, and condition
  • Compare pricing for different rental periods (monthly, quarterly, annual)
  • Read other drivers' reviews and ratings
Time Required: 5–10 minutes
2

Submit Rental Application

Select your vehicle and complete the rental agreement:

  • Choose your rental period (1 month to annual lease)
  • Review and sign the rental agreement and insurance terms
  • Provide proof of valid driver's license and insurance
  • Choose your payment method (weekly, monthly, or upfront)
  • Agree to mongoori's driver code of conduct and maintenance standards
Time Required: 10–15 minutes
3

Complete Vehicle Inspection & Handoff

Inspect and take possession of your Tesla:

  • Schedule a pickup appointment at our service center or arrange delivery
  • Walk through vehicle condition check with mongoori staff (interior, exterior, battery, systems)
  • Document baseline mileage and take photos for your records
  • Receive vehicle keys, access codes, and charging credentials
  • Get setup help for the Tesla app and mongoori driver dashboard
Time Required: 30–45 minutes
4

Start Driving with Uber/Lyft

You're ready to begin your rideshare career:

  • Ensure your Uber and Lyft driver accounts are activated and approved
  • Log into the mongoori Driver App on your Tesla's touchscreen or your phone
  • Verify your vehicle is connected and in good operating status
  • Toggle "Available" on your rideshare app and start accepting rides
  • Your first trip can happen immediately after vehicle handoff
Time to First Ride: Same day as handoff
5

Weekly & Monthly Renewal Process

Maintain your rental and maximize your earnings:

  • Weekly vehicle inspection — Check tire pressure, fluid levels, and condition before long driving days
  • Charging schedule — Plan charging during low-demand hours; mongoori covers first 100 kWh/month
  • Monthly check-in — Contact mongoori support for any maintenance or issues
  • Mileage tracking — Monitor your monthly allowance (unlimited standard miles included)
  • Renewal payments — Your rental fee renews weekly or monthly depending on your payment plan
Frequency: Weekly & monthly ongoing
