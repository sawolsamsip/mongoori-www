How to Become a mongoori Driver

1

Check Your Eligibility

To drive with mongoori, you'll need:

  • Valid California Driver's License (current, in good standing)
  • Clean driving record — no major violations in the past 3 years
  • Smartphone access (iOS or Android) to use the driver app
  • Age 21 or older (legal requirement for commercial driving)
  • Work authorization in the United States
Time Required: ~2 minutes to verify
2

Complete Your Application

The application is quick and straightforward:

  • Fill out your profile — name, contact info, driving history
  • Provide driver's license info (photo and license number)
  • Consent to a professional background check
  • Choose your payment preference (daily, weekly, or monthly)
Time Required: 5–10 minutes
3

Get Approved & Trained

Once you submit, here's what happens:

  • Background review by our team — usually within 24 hours
  • Payment setup for direct deposits to your bank
  • Short orientation video on mongoori policies and the app (10 min)
  • Optional onboarding call with the support team
  • Vehicle assignment — you're matched with a Tesla near your location
Approval Timeline: 24–48 hours
4

Start Earning

You're ready to drive:

  • Download the mongoori Driver App (iOS and Android)
  • Log in with your new driver credentials
  • Check your assigned Tesla's location in the app
  • Toggle to "Available" and start accepting ride requests
  • Earnings deposited directly to your bank account
Time to First Ride: Same day or next business day after vehicle pickup
