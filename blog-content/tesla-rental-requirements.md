## Core Requirements for Renting a Tesla from Mongoori

### 1. Age & Valid Driver's License

To rent a Tesla from Mongoori, you must be:
- **At least 21 years old** (with a valid California driver's license)
- Hold a **clean driving record** with no major violations in the past 3 years
- Have a valid license that hasn't been suspended or revoked

Your driver's license is your primary qualification document. We verify it through the California DMV system to ensure compliance with insurance and legal requirements.

### 2. Rideshare Platform Approval

You must currently be an **active, approved driver** on either:
- **Uber** (with acceptable acceptance and cancellation rates)
- **Lyft** (with comparable driver ratings and active status)

We verify your rideshare account directly through the platforms. This typically takes 1-3 business days. **Why this matters:** Rideshare platforms conduct their own background checks and continuous monitoring. This means you've already passed initial vetting. We use this verification as a trust signal and to coordinate pickup arrangements.

### 3. Income Verification

Mongoori requires proof that rideshare driving is or will be your primary income source. You'll need to provide:
- **Last 60 days of rideshare income statements** (from Uber/Lyft driver dashboards)
- **Or** recent tax returns (1040 + Schedule C) if you're a new driver

This step protects both Mongoori and you—it ensures the rental aligns with your earning capacity. Minimum monthly income requirement: **$2,000 in rideshare earnings** (either demonstrated or projected).

### 4. Background Check & Insurance

Mongoori partners with **Turner Risk Management** to conduct a comprehensive background check that includes:
- Criminal history screening (7-year lookback)
- Driving record verification
- Insurance eligibility assessment

The background check typically completes within 2-3 business days. **What disqualifies you:**
- Felony convictions (within 10 years)
- DUI/DWI convictions (within 5 years)
- Multiple traffic violations (3+ in the past year)
- Suspended or revoked license

**Insurance is automatically included** in your Mongoori rental agreement. Our policy covers commercial rideshare operation, passenger liability, and comprehensive/collision coverage.

### 5. Vehicle Agreement & Contract

Once approved, you'll sign Mongoori's **Standard Vehicle Rental Agreement**, which includes:
- **Weekly rental rate** ($299-$399/week depending on vehicle age and demand)
- **Mileage allowance** (unlimited for rideshare use)
- **Maintenance & service coverage** (Mongoori handles scheduled maintenance and roadside support)
- **Insurance & TNC coverage** (fully included in rental rate)
- **Early termination clause** (14-day notice, no early termination fees for drivers)

The agreement is straightforward and transparent. Unlike traditional car rentals, you're not paying per mile or facing surprise maintenance costs.

## Common Questions About Tesla Rental Requirements

### Do I need to have owned a car before?
No. Mongoori rental is available to drivers with zero car ownership history. What matters is your rideshare track record and income verification.

### What if my Uber/Lyft rating is below 4.8?
You'll need to wait until your rating improves or contact our driver support team. Most drivers reach 4.8+ within 2-4 weeks of consistent, quality service.

### Can I rent a Tesla if I have a recent accident on my driving record?
It depends. Minor accidents (at-fault with low damage) approved within 3+ years. Major accidents or DUI require CEO escalation. Contact our support team to discuss your specific situation.

### What insurance do I need beyond Mongoori's coverage?
None. Mongoori's policy is comprehensive for TNC rideshare operation and includes passenger liability ($1M/$2M). If you drive personal errands between trips, add personal rideshare coverage (cost: ~$15-25/month).

### Can I cancel the rental early?
Yes. Provide 14 days' notice, and you can return the vehicle without penalties. If you want to return immediately due to hardship, contact support—we work with drivers to find solutions.
