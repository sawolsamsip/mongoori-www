How It Works

Requirements to Become a Host

Vehicle Requirements

  • Tesla Model 3, Model Y, or Model S (2018+)
  • Good mechanical condition
  • Clean title (owned or financed)
  • Valid registration

Personal Requirements

  • Valid US driver's license, age 25+
  • California resident (Irvine/Orange County area)
  • Good driving record (no major violations in 5 years)
  • Tax ID or SSN for 1099 reporting

Good news: No credit check, no background check (you're the owner). Financed vehicles welcome. No special insurance needed upfront.

How to Get Started in 5 Steps

Step 1: Check Eligibility (5 min)
Your Tesla is 2018+ and in good condition, you're 25+ years old, you live in Irvine/Orange County, and you have your title and registration handy.
Step 2: Submit Application (10 min)
Visit contact@mongoori.com or call (949) 385-3271. Provide vehicle details (VIN, mileage, condition), personal info, hosting goals, and photos of your title and registration.
Step 3: Approval (24–48 hours)
mongoori reviews your vehicle, driver's license, and financed vehicle status (if applicable). You'll receive an approval email with hosting terms, an inspection appointment, and a host agreement.
Step 4: Vehicle Inspection (30 min)
Bring your ID, keys, and proof of insurance to our Irvine location. We inspect mechanical condition, mileage, and pre-existing damage.
Step 5: Launch Your Listing (Same Day)
Your Tesla goes live immediately. Set your availability and rental price ($40–$60/day) and start accepting bookings. Most hosts get their first rental within 48 hours.
