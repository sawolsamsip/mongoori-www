    Getting Started

    mongoori is a peer-to-peer Tesla rental platform built for TNC (Uber/Lyft) drivers in California. We provide flexible, long-term vehicle rentals — starting from one month — so you can drive premium Tesla vehicles without the commitment of ownership. All vehicles are insured and maintained by mongoori.

    mongoori is designed for active rideshare drivers (Uber/Lyft) who need a reliable, commercial-grade vehicle on a flexible monthly basis. If you drive 1,000+ miles per week for rideshare, you've found your solution.

    Currently serving Irvine, California. Valid TNC license and clean driving record required.

    Visit mongoori.com or download the mongoori app. Create an account with your email, then submit your driver's license and TNC credentials for verification. Most drivers are approved within 24–48 hours. Once approved, you can book a Tesla and start driving immediately.

    You must meet ALL of the following:

    • At least 21 years old
    • Valid U.S. driver's license (or international license with valid U.S. visa)
    • Active TNC driver license (Uber/Lyft registration confirmed)
    • Clean driving record (no major violations in the past 3 years)
    • Valid California address
    • Acceptance of our Terms of Service and rental agreement

    mongoori is currently available in Irvine, California. We plan to expand to additional California regions in 2026. For current availability, check our app or visit mongoori.com.

    Booking & Reservations

    Visit mongoori.com or use the mongoori app. Search by month and location (currently Irvine, CA). Select your vehicle, review the pricing and terms, and confirm your booking. Your rental begins on the first day of the month and is charged upfront. You'll receive pickup instructions via email and in the app.

    mongoori specializes in multi-month rentals, starting from 1 month. Rental periods are standardized by calendar month (1st through last day). Extensions are available if the vehicle isn't reserved by another driver.

    Yes, cancellation is allowed, but your refund depends on when you cancel. Our 5-tier cancellation policy is disclosed in your rental agreement:

    TierTimingRefund
    Tier 1 Within 1 hour of booking AND 72+ hours before rental start 100% (full amount)
    Tier 2 More than 72 hours before rental start 100% (full amount)
    Tier 3 24–72 hours before rental start 50% of rental fee — platform service fee non-refundable
    Tier 4 Less than 24 hours before rental start No refund (0%)
    Tier 5 After rental starts, including no-show No refund. Exception: vehicle defects or platform errors — contact support.

    Refunds are processed within 3–5 business days.

    You'll authorize a $500 security deposit on your credit card at booking. This is NOT charged upfront — it's held as a guarantee. The hold is released within 3–5 business days after your rental ends (assuming no damage or outstanding charges).

    Month-to-month rentals are fixed by calendar month. If you need to shorten or extend, contact mongoori support via the app or at support@mongoori.com. Extensions are subject to vehicle availability.

    Getting Started

    A mongoori Host is a vehicle owner who rents their Tesla to verified TNC drivers on a monthly basis. You set your own price, manage availability, and earn money directly from drivers who need reliable vehicles for rideshare work. mongoori handles insurance, payments, and support — you focus on vehicle condition and earnings.

    Visit mongoori.com/host or use the mongoori app. Click "List Your Vehicle" and submit:

    • Vehicle details (year, model, mileage, title)
    • 5–10 photos (exterior, interior, odometer)
    • Proof of ownership and title
    • Current insurance documentation
    • Bank account for payouts

    Most vehicles are approved within 1–3 business days. Once approved, your listing goes live and drivers can book.

    Tesla vehicles only. Eligible vehicles must be:

    • 100% battery electric Tesla (Model 3, Model Y, Model S, or Model X)
    • Model year 2020 or newer preferred (2018+ considered)
    • Clean title (no salvage or rebuilt titles)
    • Under 100k miles preferred
    • Insured with valid personal or commercial auto policy
    • No mechanical issues or outstanding recalls

    Pricing & Earnings

    You set your own monthly rate in your host dashboard. mongoori provides pricing benchmarks based on vehicle model, age, condition, and location. A 2023 Tesla Model 3 in Irvine typically rents for $1,800–$2,200/month. You can adjust your price monthly or on demand.

    Example at a $2,000/month rental:

    • Gross revenue: $2,000
    • mongoori platform fee (25%): $500
    • Your net earnings: $1,500/month
    • Vehicle costs (estimated): Depreciation, insurance, maintenance ~$300–500/month
    • Your profit: ~$1,000–1,200/month (varies by vehicle and utilization)

    Earnings are deposited via ACH direct transfer to your bank account within 1–2 business days after each monthly rental ends. You'll receive a detailed earnings statement showing gross revenue, mongoori's platform fee, and your net payout.

    mongoori charges a 25% platform fee on all monthly rental revenue. This covers:

    • Insurance (liability + physical damage for all drivers)
    • Payment processing and collections
    • Host customer support (disputes, damage claims)
    • Platform operations and maintenance
    • Marketing (to attract drivers)

    The 25% fee is transparent and clearly shown before you set your price.
