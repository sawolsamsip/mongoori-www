Earnings & Payment

How much can I earn with mongoori?

Most mongoori drivers earn between $1,200–$1,800 per week, depending on how many hours you drive and where. Top performers consistently earn $2,000+.

Your actual earnings depend on:

  • Hours driven — More hours = more rides = more earnings
  • Location — High-demand areas (downtown LA, airport routes) pay more
  • Time of day — Peak hours (mornings, evenings) have surge pricing (1.5x–2x rates)
  • Your rating — Higher-rated drivers get priority for premium rides
  • Tips — Passengers can tip after every ride; you keep 100%

Example: Drive 25 hours/week during peak times and earn $1,400–$1,600. Work weekends only (12 hours) and earn $600–$800.

When and how do I get paid?

You choose your payout schedule:

  • Daily Deposit — Cash out anytime (small fee of 1–2% applies). Best if you need money fast.
  • Weekly Automatic Deposit — Free transfer every Monday. Predictable, routine paychecks.
  • Monthly Deposit — Free transfer on the 1st of each month. Fewer transactions, great for budgeting.

All payments go directly to your bank account via ACH transfer. No checks, no delays, no middlemen.

Are there bonuses or incentives?

  • Referral Bonuses — Earn $100–$500 when you refer another driver who completes 25 rides
  • Surge Pricing — During peak hours (7–9 AM, 5–7 PM weekdays), you earn the full surge amount. No cut taken by mongoori.
  • Streak Bonuses — Complete rides during peak hours for extra rewards (e.g., 5 peak-hour rides = $25 bonus)
  • Loyalty Rewards — Long-term drivers (6+ months) get priority for premium rides and milestone bonuses
  • Tips — 100% of passenger tips go to you

What about taxes and deductions?

You're an independent contractor, so taxes are your responsibility. Key points:

  • You're responsible for federal and California income tax
  • We recommend setting aside 25–30% of earnings for taxes
  • We provide detailed earnings statements and 1099 forms for tax filing
  • Vehicle expenses may be deductible — consult a tax professional familiar with gig work

Vehicle & Operations

Do I need my own car to drive with mongoori?

No. mongoori provides all vehicles. Every driver gets access to a premium Tesla Model 3, Model Y, or Model S. No car payment, no insurance cost, no maintenance burden — that's all on us. You cannot use your own vehicle.

What if I get a flat tire or the car breaks down?

You're covered. Here's exactly what happens:

  • Press the SOS button in your car (or contact support via the app)
  • Our team connects with you right away and coordinates roadside assistance or towing
  • For flat tires: our roadside team arrives within 15–30 minutes
  • For major breakdowns: a replacement Tesla arrives within 30–60 minutes

You never pay for towing, repairs, roadside assistance, or vehicle replacement. That's our job.

What's the vehicle pickup and return process?

Pickup: Your assigned Tesla's location is sent to your phone. Drive to the lot, complete a digital sign-off in the app, and you're set. Takes about 15 minutes on your first pickup.

Return: Drive to our lot, park in the designated spot, and check the vehicle condition in the app. Earnings processing begins immediately.

Pickup hours: 6 AM–10 PM daily. After-hours pickups are available for verified drivers.

What if I return the vehicle late?

Late returns are charged at $75 per day. Please return the vehicle on time or contact support in advance if you need an extension — we'll do our best to accommodate you.

To avoid late fees, use the in-app return scheduler and notify support at least 2 hours before your expected return time if plans change.

What's covered by insurance?

All mongoori Teslas are fully insured with $0 deductible for you:

  • Collision damage (accidents with other vehicles or objects)
  • Liability (injuries to passengers or third parties)
  • Medical coverage (injuries in your vehicle)
  • Theft and vandalism
  • 24/7 roadside assistance (towing, lockout, tire changes)

Not covered: Traffic violations, parking tickets, or damage from intentional or illegal activities.

Charging Your Tesla

Who pays for Supercharger sessions?

mongoori covers all standard charging costs when you use designated Tesla Superchargers on the approved list in your driver app. No out-of-pocket charging expenses for you during active shifts.

For reference, representative Supercharger rates in Southern California (e.g., Irvine area) run approximately $0.50/kWh at peak times, with off-peak rates as low as $0.40–$0.47/kWh. You don't need to track these costs — we handle it.

How do I charge the Tesla during my shift?

Your driver app shows nearby approved Supercharger locations. Simply navigate there when the battery drops below 20%. The charge session is logged automatically through the vehicle — no payment action needed from you.

Tips for efficient charging:

  • Charge during slow periods (mid-morning, early afternoon) to minimize earnings interruption
  • A 20-minute Supercharger session adds 100–150 miles of range
  • The app alerts you when battery is getting low

What happens if I get in an accident?

  1. Pull over safely — Get out of traffic if possible
  2. Contact support — Use the SOS button or in-app emergency feature
  3. Call 911 if needed — For injuries or major damage
  4. Take photos — Document damage to both vehicles and the scene

mongoori's support team checks on you, coordinates towing, arranges a replacement Tesla, and handles all insurance claims. Your cost: $0.

How does mongoori handle driver safety?

Safety is built into every vehicle and process:

  • Dual Cameras — Inside and outside, protecting you during disputes
  • Emergency SOS Button — Direct connection to support
  • Real-Time GPS — Your location always known by support
  • Automatic Crash Detection — Alerts support instantly
  • Passenger ID Verification — All riders must verify identity
  • Driver Can Refuse — Cancel any ride without penalty

What if a passenger makes a complaint?

mongoori investigates all complaints fairly. You'll be notified, given full details, and have the opportunity to respond with your account. Support reviews in-car camera footage and makes a decision within 24–48 hours. You can appeal any decision you disagree with.

Scheduling & Flexibility

Can I work whenever I want?

Yes, completely. No minimum hours, no shift requirements. Open the mongoori app, toggle "Available," and start accepting requests. Turn off anytime. Work 5 hours this week and 50 hours next — no questions asked.

Can I also drive for Uber, Lyft, or other platforms?

Yes. Many mongoori drivers work on multiple platforms. mongoori has no exclusivity clause — work where you want. Just coordinate your vehicle schedules and ensure insurance compliance for any non-mongoori vehicles.

Can I take time off?

Yes. No penalties, no approval needed. Stop accepting rides when you're done for the day, pause for a week, or take a month off. Your account stays active and you can return whenever you're ready — no re-qualification required.

Do I need a California driver's license?

Yes. A valid, current California driver's license is required — no major violations in the past 3 years, not suspended or revoked. If you recently moved to California, you'll need to obtain a CA license (typically 4–6 weeks through the DMV) before applying.

Do I need my own insurance?

No. mongoori's insurance covers you fully when driving a mongoori vehicle — liability, collision, medical payments, and comprehensive coverage. You do not need personal rideshare insurance for mongoori vehicles. If you drive other cars on other platforms, you'll need separate coverage for those.
