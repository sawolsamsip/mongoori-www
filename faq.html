Earnings & Payment

How much can I earn with mongoori?

Most mongoori drivers earn between $1,200–$1,800 per week, depending on how many hours you drive and where. Top performers consistently earn $2,000+.

Your actual earnings depend on:

  • Hours driven — More hours = more rides = more earnings
  • Location — High-demand areas (downtown LA, airport routes) pay more
  • Time of day — Peak hours (mornings, evenings) have surge pricing (1.5x–2x rates)
  • Your rating — Higher-rated drivers get priority for premium rides
  • Tips — Passengers can tip after every ride; you keep 100%

Example: Drive 25 hours/week during peak times and earn $1,400–$1,600. Work weekends only (12 hours) and earn $600–$800.

When and how do I get paid?

You choose your payout schedule:

  • Daily Deposit — Cash out anytime (small fee of 1–2% applies). Best if you need money fast.
  • Weekly Automatic Deposit — Free transfer every Monday. Predictable, routine paychecks.
  • Monthly Deposit — Free transfer on the 1st of each month. Fewer transactions, great for budgeting.

All payments go directly to your bank account via ACH transfer. No checks, no delays, no middlemen.

Are there bonuses or incentives?

  • Referral Bonuses — Earn $100–$500 when you refer another driver who completes 25 rides
  • Surge Pricing — During peak hours (7–9 AM, 5–7 PM weekdays), you earn the full surge amount. No cut taken by mongoori.
  • Streak Bonuses — Complete rides during peak hours for extra rewards (e.g., 5 peak-hour rides = $25 bonus)
  • Loyalty Rewards — Long-term drivers (6+ months) get priority for premium rides and milestone bonuses
  • Tips — 100% of passenger tips go to you

What about taxes and deductions?

You're an independent contractor, so taxes are your responsibility. Here's what you need to know:

Self-Employment Tax:

  • You're responsible for federal and California income tax
  • We provide earnings statements and 1099 forms for tax filing
  • We recommend setting aside 25–30% of earnings for taxes

Deductions:

  • Vehicle expenses (mileage, maintenance, insurance) may be deductible
  • Check with a tax professional or accountant
  • Keep records of all work-related expenses

Our Help: We provide detailed earnings reports showing gross and net, monthly breakdown by income type, and tax-ready documents (1099-NEC or 1099-MISC).

Vehicle & Operations

Do I need my own car to drive with mongoori?

No. mongoori provides all vehicles. Every driver gets access to a premium Tesla Model 3, Model Y, or Model S. No car payment, no insurance cost, no maintenance burden — that's all on us. You cannot use your own vehicle.

What if I get a flat tire or the car breaks down?

You're covered. Here's exactly what happens:

  • Press the SOS button in your car (or contact support via the app)
  • Our team connects with you right away and coordinates roadside assistance or towing
  • For flat tires: our roadside team arrives within 15–30 minutes
  • For major breakdowns: a replacement Tesla arrives within 30–60 minutes

You never pay for towing, repairs, roadside assistance, or vehicle replacement. That's our job.

Can I drive my own car instead?

Not currently. mongoori only operates with our provided Tesla fleet. This ensures:

  • Consistent quality for every passenger
  • Fair insurance coverage for everyone
  • Professional vehicle standards
  • Zero maintenance burden on you

We may offer owner-operator programs in the future. Check back in 2027.

What's the vehicle assignment and pickup process?

Getting Your Vehicle:

  1. You're assigned a Tesla based on your location and availability
  2. Location details sent to your phone
  3. You drive to the designated lot and pick it up
  4. All paperwork handled in the app (digital sign-off)
  5. Vehicle is fueled and ready to drive

Timing: Assignment usually same day as approval. Pickup: same day or next business day at your convenience. Process takes about 15 minutes (first time takes longer).

Drop-Off: Drive to our lot when you're done, park in the designated spot, check the vehicle condition in the app. Earnings processing begins immediately.

Flexibility: You can return vehicles anytime. Pickup hours: 6 AM–10 PM daily. After-hours pickups available for verified drivers.

What if I return the vehicle late?

Late returns are charged at $25 per day. Please return the vehicle on time or contact support in advance if you need an extension — we'll do our best to accommodate you.

To avoid late fees, use the in-app return scheduler and notify support at least 2 hours before your expected return time if plans change.

What's covered by insurance?

All mongoori Teslas are fully insured with $0 deductible for you:

  • Collision damage (accidents with other vehicles or objects)
  • Liability (injuries to passengers or third parties)
  • Medical coverage (injuries in your vehicle)
  • Theft and vandalism
  • 24/7 roadside assistance (towing, lockout, tire changes)

Not covered: Traffic violations, parking tickets, or damage from intentional or illegal activities.

Charging Your Tesla

Who pays for Supercharger sessions?

mongoori covers all standard charging costs when you use designated Tesla Superchargers on the approved list in your driver app. No out-of-pocket charging expenses for you during active shifts.

For reference, representative Supercharger rates in Southern California (e.g., Irvine area) run approximately $0.50/kWh at peak times, with off-peak rates as low as $0.40–$0.47/kWh. You don't need to track these costs — we handle it.

How do I charge the Tesla during my shift?

Your driver app shows nearby approved Supercharger locations. Simply navigate there when the battery drops below 20%. The charge session is logged automatically through the vehicle — no payment action needed from you.

Tips for efficient charging:

  • Charge during slow periods (mid-morning, early afternoon) to minimize earnings interruption
  • A 20-minute Supercharger session adds 100–150 miles of range
  • The app alerts you when battery is getting low

Safety is built into every vehicle and process:

  • Automatic Crash Detection — Alerts support instantly
  • Passenger ID Verification — All riders must verify identity

What if a passenger has a problem or makes accusations?

mongoori investigates all complaints fairly:

  1. Passenger files a complaint in the app
  2. You're notified immediately and given the details
  3. You can respond with your account

Scheduling & Flexibility

Can I work whenever I want?

Yes, completely. No minimum hours, no shift requirements. Open the mongoori app, toggle "Available," and start accepting requests. Turn off anytime. Work 5 hours this week and 50 hours next — no questions asked.

Can I also drive for Uber, Lyft, or other platforms?

Yes. Many mongoori drivers work on multiple platforms. mongoori has no exclusivity clause — work where you want. Just coordinate your vehicle schedules and ensure insurance compliance for any non-mongoori vehicles.

Can I take time off?

Yes. No penalties, no approval needed.

Time Off Policies:

  • Just stop accepting rides when you're done
  • You can pause your availability at any time
  • No minimum shift completion required
  • No consequences for gaps in activity

Taking Extended Time Off:

  • 1–2 weeks off? Just don't drive. No problem.
  • 1+ months off? Let support know, but no penalty
  • Returning after a long break? Jump back in anytime

Can I earn on multiple platforms?

Yes. Many mongoori drivers also work for Uber, Lyft, DoorDash, or other services.

Multi-Platform Success:

  • Stagger your apps (use one, then the other)
  • Keep track of vehicle/shift rules
  • Some drivers earn $100+ per hour with strategic multi-apping
  • Maximize earning by following each platform's peak times

What You Should Know:

  • You must have insurance for every car you drive
  • Vehicle mileage limits may apply (check lease terms)
  • Each platform has conduct requirements (follow all of them)

mongoori's Position: We don't restrict multi-apping. Drive with Uber and mongoori simultaneously. Use mongoori and DoorDash together. No exclusivity clause — work where you want.

What if I'm in an accident and can't drive for a few days?

mongoori supports you through recovery:

What Happens: Use the app or call to notify support. Briefly explain the situation. Take time to recover (no pressure). Return when you're ready.

If It's a mongoori Vehicle Issue: We provide a replacement immediately. No lost earnings. No penalties for the downtime.

If It's Your Personal Issue: Time off is fine — no consequences. Your account remains active. Resume when ready, no re-qualification needed.

Insurance/Medical: Injuries from accidents are covered by mongoori insurance. Consult a lawyer for your rights as a gig worker.

Location & Requirements

Where does mongoori operate?

Live now:

  • Los Angeles (LA metro area)
  • Orange County (Irvine, Santa Ana, Costa Mesa, Newport Beach)
  • San Diego metro area

Coming soon (2026–2027): Inland Empire, Ventura County, San Francisco Bay Area

Do I need a California driver's license?

Yes. A valid, current California driver's license is required — no major violations in the past 3 years, not suspended or revoked. If you recently moved to California, you'll need to obtain a CA license (typically 4–6 weeks through the DMV) before applying.

Can I drive if I have an international license?

No. California law requires a valid CA driver's license for commercial driving.

If You're an International Driver:

  1. You'll need a work visa/authorization
  2. Get a California driver's license (apply at DMV)
  3. Once approved, apply to mongoori

Timeline: Typically 4–6 weeks to get your CA license through the DMV.

Is there insurance I need to carry personally?

No. mongoori's insurance covers you when you're driving a mongoori vehicle.

What mongoori Covers:

  • Liability (injuries to passengers/others)
  • Collision (damage to vehicles)
  • Medical payments (injuries in the car)
  • Comprehensive (theft, vandalism, natural disasters)

What You Don't Need: Personal rideshare insurance (mongoori provides it). Commercial auto insurance (mongoori provides it). Medical insurance for passengers (mongoori provides it).

Important Note: This coverage only applies to mongoori vehicles. If you drive other cars (Uber, etc.), you need separate insurance for those.
