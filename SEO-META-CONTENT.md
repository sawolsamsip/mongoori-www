#### Secondary Pages (Hosts/Vehicle Owners)
```

/host-earnings            → Host earning potential
```

#### Supporting Pages
```
/about                    → Company background
/contact                  → Contact form
/privacy                  → Privacy policy

```

### Recommended Slug Naming Convention:
- ✅ Use hyphens (not underscores or camelCase)
- ✅ Keep slugs short and keyword-rich
- ✅ Avoid numbers, unnecessary words
- ✅ Use `/for-drivers` and `/for-hosts` to segment audiences clearly
